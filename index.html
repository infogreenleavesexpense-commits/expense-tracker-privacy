Your Privacy is Our Priority

We operate on an Offline-First basis. Your personal financial data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Green Leaves: Receipt & Expense Tracker ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use our mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have full control over your financial data.

Core Privacy Principle: We operate on an "Offline-First" basis. Your personal financial data (expenses, receipts, categories) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

  • Expense Data: Amounts, dates, merchant names, categories, and descriptions you enter manually.
  • Receipt Images: Photos of receipts you capture via the camera or import from your gallery.
  • Preferences: Settings such as currency, dark mode, and notification preferences.

B. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and general diagnostic data to ensure app stability.
  • Crash Logs: Anonymous crash reports (via Google Play Console) to help us fix bugs.
  • Ad Data: If you use the free version, our advertising partner (Google AdMob) may collect device identifiers (advertising ID) to serve non-personalized or personalized ads based on your consent.

3. How We Use Your Information

Data Type Purpose Processing Location
Receipt Images To extract text (Amount, Date, Merchant) using Optical Character Recognition (OCR). On-Device Only (via Google ML Kit). Images are NOT uploaded to our servers.
Financial Data To organize your expenses, generate charts, and calculate totals. On-Device Only (Stored in local Room Database).
Advertising ID To display relevant advertisements in the free version. Processed by Google AdMob.
Purchase History To verify your Premium Subscription status. Processed by Google Play Billing.

4. Permissions We Request

  • CAMERA: Required solely to capture photos of receipts for scanning.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Required to send you local reminders to log your expenses. You can disable this in device settings.
  • INTERNET: Required for:
    • Loading advertisements (AdMob).
    • Verifying subscriptions (Google Play Billing).
    • Downloading crash reports (Play Console).

Note: Your expense data is NOT sent over the internet.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services: For core app functionality.
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. Privacy Policy
  • Google ML Kit: For on-device text recognition. Data is processed locally. Privacy Policy

6. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Retention: Since data is stored locally, it remains on your device as long as the App is installed.
  • Deletion: You can delete all your data by simply uninstalling the App or clearing the App storage in system settings.
  • Backup: If you use the "Export to CSV" feature, the exported file is under your control and subject to your own management.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
